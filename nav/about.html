About

Course Directory is a student-fueled portal to all the courses for York College of Pennsylvania. Feel free to use this site to see your future course requirements, do your assignments ahead of time, or just to avoid using tons of bookmarks for your classes.

FAQ

A course I'm looking for isn't listed. Why?

Either the course doesn't exist, or someone hasn't sent it to me yet. To add a course, fill out the form on the "contribute" tab.

This is awesome! How can I help?

If you want to help with the upkeep of the site and are pretty computer-savvy, reach out to Admin@CourseDirectory.space. If you see some courses are missing from the site, check out "contribute" on our site. If you want to buy a burrito for me, my Venmo is @pay-here.

Takedown Requests

If you're a professor or school administrator who wants any links posted taken down, please email Admin@CourseDirectory.space and we'll get your situation sorted.
